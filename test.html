High Performance Dry ice Bucket

Overview

The NovaCellBio High Performance Dry Ice Bucket is a specialized laboratory-grade storage solution designed to safely and efficiently conserve dry ice and other ultra-low temperature materials. Engineered for professional scientific, medical, and research applications, this durable container ensures optimal performance, reduced wastage, and user safety.

Key Features
Superior Insulation Technology

The NovaCellBio bucket features advanced multi-layer insulation construction that provides exceptional cryogenic performance. Its innovative design enables maintaining low temperatures for extended periods, significantly reducing dry ice sublimation rates and preserving truly valuable samples.

Chemical-Resistant Construction

Manufactured from high-grade materials with superior chemical resistance, this bucket is compatible not only with dry ice but also with various laboratory solutions including:

  • Safe flammable materials
  • Acidic solutions
  • Alkaline solutions
  • Other cryogenic materials
High-Performance Design
  • Non-woven design prevents condensation buildup on the outer surface
  • Ergonomically designed for ease of handling and storage
  • Specialized system ensures maximum retention and minimizes sublimation loss
